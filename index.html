PROJECTS

My Outkast (Popup)

A chrome extension that allows holders of the "outkast" nft to track each and every nft that they own. It also gives them the ability to view stats about the project such as the holders, the floor price, and the amount of "shards" (currency) they own. On top of that they can view the current missions and see if their outkasts are deployed or not in a glance.

My Outkast (Squad Creator)

An additional function to the chrome extension that modifies the "outkast" nft website in order to add additional functionality such as creating preset groups, and exporting/importing those groups. Using the extension allows users to send all their outkasts on missions 4 times faster than it takes to send them on missions without the extension.
